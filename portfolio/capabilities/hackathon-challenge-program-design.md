# Hackathon and Challenge Program Design

## Status

Current and past capability.

## What it solves

Hackathons and innovation challenges often create activity without follow-up.
This capability designs challenge programs as structured pipelines for evidence,
prototypes, talent discovery, partner formation, and post-event implementation.

## Technical work

- Challenge framing.
- Vertical design.
- Sponsor and partner packages.
- Participant onboarding.
- Mentor and judge workflows.
- Prototype templates.
- Evidence and pitch formats.
- Evaluation rubrics.
- Post-event project pipeline.
- Follow-up contracting or incubation route.

## Current applications

- Startup-community and AI challenge design.
- LabLab.ai-related experience.
- Techstars Startup Weekend AI4ALL CDMX.
- Corporate innovation and internal challenge proposals.
- Public-sector and university innovation routes.

## Past foundations

- Enterprise innovation and hackathon delivery.
- MassChallenge and MIT-related mentoring contexts.
- Cloud, AI, SAP, IoT, and big-data proof-of-concept work.
