# Complex Systems, Simulation, and Scenario Design

## Status

Current and past capability.

## What it solves

Many civic and institutional problems are not linear. They involve actors,
constraints, spatial structure, service rules, feedback loops, uncertainty, and
policy trade-offs. This capability translates those problems into models and
scenario workflows that people can understand and test.

## Technical work

- Agent-based modeling.
- System dynamics framing.
- Scenario variables and intervention design.
- Actor, rule, and constraint modeling.
- Service-pressure modeling.
- Socio-physical model design.
- Operational and policy outcome definition.

## Typical model objects

- actors;
- places;
- assets;
- flows;
- service rules;
- constraints;
- interventions;
- pressure indicators;
- evaluation criteria.
