# CL-GOV-003 - HPC Diagnostic and Regional Advanced-Computing Roadmap

## Public title

High-performance computing diagnostic and business-model roadmap for regional
innovation.

## Client status

Anonymized public-sector / research institution context.

## Role

Lead researcher.

## Problem

Regional high-performance computing capacity can remain underused when
institutions do not have a clear adoption model, business model, industrial
pipeline, talent strategy, or connection between research computing and
productive-sector needs.

## Technical approach

The work studied how supercomputing could support regional economic development,
industrial modernization, research capability, and advanced software formation.
The analysis connected technical capacity with institutional models and
implementation pathways.

## Themes

- HPC adoption strategy.
- Industrial and research use cases.
- Talent and certification needs.
- Advanced software laboratory concept.
- Public-private collaboration model.
- Five-year roadmap.
- Potential workloads in manufacturing, bioinformatics, materials, AI, and
  geospatial/digital twin systems.

## Current extension

The line now connects to digital twin workloads: geospatial tiling, OSM network
extraction, simulation sweeps, batch experiments, large evidence processing, and
parallel computation using OpenMPI or related approaches.
