# Product Systems

## Polisplexity

Public surface:

- https://polisplexity.tech
- https://github.com/polisplexity

Purpose:

Polisplexity builds local digital twin infrastructure for municipal
transformation. It combines geospatial data, AI agents, simulation, semantic
city layers, and civic workflows for planning, resilience, mobility, energy,
waste, and public operations.

Current product lines:

- Open Local Digital Twin.
- Twin Base Studio.
- Energy Twin.
- Socio-Physical Digital Twin for Waste.
- City-fragment XR / 3D analytical interfaces.
- Planning-ready city twin workflows.
- Interoperability and semantic city data routes.

## Hadox Research Labs

Public surface:

- https://hadox.org

- Country and sector intelligence surfaces.

- Evidence vaults.

## EVA Breast Cancer Archive Platform

Public repository:

- https://github.com/Hadox-Research-Labs/eva-hadox-org

Purpose:

An interdisciplinary research platform for the history of medicine, digital
humanities, terminology, classification, and corpus exploration around pre-1900
breast cancer sources.

Technical elements:

- Curated seed corpus.
- Editable lexicon of historical terms and variants.
- Full-corpus mention detection.
- TF-IDF contextual similarity.
- Collaborative document ingestion.
- Research methodology documentation.

## urban-sami

Public repository:

- https://github.com/ekaropolus/urban-sami

Purpose:

Headless research-first workflow engine for urban scaling, SAMI, and
Polisplexity parity experiments.

Technical elements:

- Workflow manifests.
- Model fitting kernels.
- Summary-matrix replay.
- Parity workflows.
- Monograph-to-code mapping.
- Equation traceability.
- Postgres / PostGIS experiment database.
- INEGI, DENUE, OSM, city, state, AGEB, and manzana support workflows.
