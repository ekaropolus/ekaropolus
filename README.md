# Edgar Antonio Valdes Porras

**Complexity physicist, civic AI builder, and founder working on scientific intelligence infrastructure.**

I build systems where physics, AI, data engineering, public policy, and city operations meet. My current work is organized around three connected fronts:

- **Polisplexity:** local digital twins, AI agents, and simulation infrastructure for cities.
- **Hadox Research Labs:** applied research infrastructure for scientific intelligence, civic data, and institutional decision-making.
- **Ekaropolus:** my personal laboratory for prototypes, research code, public experiments, and early technical work.

## Current Direction

I am focused on building reusable infrastructure for complex institutions and cities:

- local digital twins and semantic city layers
- AI agents for public policy, research, and operations
- graph and network models for civic and scientific ecosystems
- high-performance computing and quantum technology strategy
- research archives, source ledgers, and evidence-backed intelligence systems
- cloud, DevOps, data engineering, and automation for scientific organizations

## Selected Themes

**City Intelligence**

Polisplexity builds tools for municipal transformation: digital twins, AI agents, simulations, and data-driven workflows for planning, resilience, mobility, environment, and operations.

**Scientific Intelligence**

Hadox Research Labs develops systems that help institutions organize evidence, map ecosystems, evaluate opportunities, and turn research material into usable intelligence.

**Complex Systems**

My background is in physics, sustainability, computational systems, network theory, and mathematical modeling. I use that lens to design software for policy, infrastructure, research, and institutional strategy.
